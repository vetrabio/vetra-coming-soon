As we live longer and healthier, so should our beloved companion animals.

Ageing is not just time. It's states. We’re building AI-driven biology to understand how animals age — and how those trajectories can shift.

Built in Cambridge at the intersection of computational modelling, molecular epigenetics and translational science

What we do

Vetra Bio is assembling the data + modelling stack needed to read biological state in animals, and to forecast how that state changes across time, environment, and interventions.

We’re starting with predictive capability first — building the foundation for smarter studies, stronger partnerships, and, later, precise therapeutics.

If you’re a veterinary group, diagnostics company, or research partner with relevant cohorts or samples, we’d love to explore collaboration.
